Scope & Purpose ISO 9001:2015 | ISO 14001:2015 | ISO 45001:2018 | ISO 50001:2018 ▾
Purpose

This IMS Manual is the apex document of the Yash Poly Plast Integrated Management System. It defines organisational context, leadership commitment, scope, and clause-by-clause conformance to ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and ISO 50001:2018. All lower-tier documents — SOPs, Work Instructions, Forms, and Registers — derive their authority from this manual.

Scope of IMS

Yash Poly Plast designs, manufactures, and supplies HDPE Bottles and Cans for industrial and consumer applications. The IMS covers blow moulding, injection moulding, raw material storage, quality control, maintenance, warehousing, and despatch at the Navi Mumbai facility. Clause 8.3 (Design and Development) is excluded — products are manufactured to customer-specified designs.

Applies To

All employees, contractors, and visitors at the Yash Poly Plast facility. Applicable to all departments: Production, Quality, Maintenance, Purchase, Stores, Warehouse, and Administration.

Management Representative

PL Pradhan is appointed as Management Representative with full authority for IMS implementation, maintenance, and continual improvement. Reports directly to the Director (Tarun Mishra).

Process Approach & PDCA Cycle ISO 9001:2015 §0.3 ▾

Yash Poly Plast adopts the Process Approach as required by ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and ISO 50001:2018. All activities are managed as interrelated processes within the PDCA (Plan-Do-Check-Act) cycle to achieve consistent and predictable results.

📋 Plan

Establish IMS objectives; identify environmental aspects, hazards, and energy uses; determine legal obligations; and plan processes to deliver results aligned with customer requirements and interested-party expectations.

⚙️ Do

Implement and execute planned processes — production, quality control, supplier management, training, housekeeping, waste management, energy monitoring — according to documented SOPs and Work Instructions.

🔍 Check

Monitor and measure process performance against policies, objectives, and legal requirements through internal audits, management reviews, calibration, customer feedback, environmental monitoring, and energy performance indicators.

📈 Act

Take corrective and preventive actions; drive continual improvement through CAPA, management review outputs, and energy performance reviews to enhance IMS effectiveness.

Policy Statement ▾
Policy Statement

Yash Poly Plast is committed to delivering high-quality HDPE Bottles and Cans while protecting the environment and ensuring the health and safety of every employee.

Our Integrated Management System (IMS) is established in conformance with ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and ISO 50001:2018.

We pledge to: meet customer requirements and continually improve product quality; prevent pollution and minimise our environmental footprint; eliminate hazards and reduce OH&S risks to our workforce; improve energy performance and reduce energy intensity; comply with all applicable legal and regulatory requirements; and engage all employees in the continual improvement of our management systems.

4.0 Context of the Organisation ▾
4.1Internal and External Issues

Yash Poly Plast has systematically determined the issues relevant to its purpose and strategic direction.

  • Internal: production capacity, workforce competence, machine reliability, energy efficiency, financial resources
  • External: customer expectations (Arabian Petroleum, Apar Industries, Exsan Industries), raw material price volatility
  • External: regulatory requirements under Factories Act and Environment Protection Act
  • External: competitor landscape, community obligations, and environmental context
4.2Interested Parties

The following interested parties and their requirements are documented and reviewed at each Management Review Meeting:

  • Customers — product quality, on-time delivery, traceability
  • Employees — safe working conditions, competence, fair treatment
  • Regulatory bodies — compliance with Factories Act, EPA, BIS standards
  • Suppliers — clear specifications, timely payment, long-term partnership
  • Local community — minimal environmental impact, noise, and waste
4.3Scope of the IMS

The IMS scope covers: blow moulding, injection moulding, raw material storage, quality control, maintenance, warehousing, and despatch at the Navi Mumbai facility. Clause 8.3 (Design and Development) is excluded as products are manufactured to customer-specified designs.

4.4IMS Processes

Process interactions are mapped in the Process Interaction Matrix. Each process has a defined owner, inputs, outputs, KPIs, and associated documented information. Refer to the Process List in this manual.

6.0 Planning ▾
6.1Risks and Opportunities

Planning addresses risks and opportunities across all four management systems:

  • OH&S risks: Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment — YPP_SOP-OHS-001
  • Environmental risks: Aspects & Impacts evaluation — YPP_SOP-ENV-001
  • Quality risks: Risk-Based Thinking register — YPP_SOP-RBI-001
  • Legal compliance: Legal registers — YPP_REG-LGL-01, YPP_REG-LGL-OHS-01
6.2IMS Objectives and Planning

IMS objectives are established for each standard, covering quality, safety, environment, and energy. Objectives are SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound). Tracked via YPP_REG-OBJ-01 and reviewed at Management Review Meetings. Refer to the Objectives table in this manual.

6.3Energy Planning

The Energy Review identifies Significant Energy Uses (SEUs), energy performance baselines, and Energy Performance Indicators (EnPIs). Energy planning is documented in YPP_SOP-SEU-001 and tracked through YPP_REG-ENM-01.

6.4Planning for Change

Planned changes to the IMS are assessed for risk and opportunity before implementation in accordance with the Change Management process described in this manual and YPP_SOP-DC-001.

7.0 Support ▾
7.1Resources

The organisation ensures adequate resources for IMS operation including people, infrastructure, work environment, monitoring and measurement equipment, and organisational knowledge. Infrastructure and work environment are maintained per YPP_SOP-MTN-001 and YPP_SOP-HK-001.

7.2Competence and Training

Employees performing work that affects IMS performance are required to be competent on the basis of education, training, or experience.

  • Competence requirements and training needs analysis — YPP_SOP-COM-001
  • Training records maintained in — YPP_REG-TRN-01
  • Training effectiveness evaluated after each training event
  • New employee IMS induction completed within 30 days of joining
7.3Awareness

All employees are made aware of IMS policies, their contribution to IMS objectives, and the consequences of not conforming to IMS requirements. Awareness is reinforced through toolbox talks, induction training, and notice boards.

7.4Monitoring, Measurement & Calibration

Measurement equipment is identified, calibrated, and maintained per YPP_SOP-CAL-001. Calibration records are maintained in YPP_REG-CAL-01. Equipment found out-of-calibration is quarantined and previous results assessed for validity.

7.5Documented Information

Document and record control follows YPP_SOP-DC-001. The Document Master List is maintained by the MR. Documents are reviewed and approved before issue, and updated when changes occur.

8.0 Operation ▾
8.1Production Control

Blow moulding and injection moulding operations are controlled through documented Work Instructions and SOPs:

  • Production SOP — YPP_SOP-PRD-001
  • Work Instructions — YPP_WI_PRD-01 to YPP_WI_PRD-07
  • In-process quality control — YPP_SOP-IPC-001
  • Final quality control — YPP_SOP-FQC-001
8.2Purchasing and Supplier Control

Supplier evaluation, selection, and performance monitoring are governed by YPP_SOP-PUR-001 and YPP_SOP-SUP-001. Incoming materials are inspected per YPP_WI_IQC-01 and results recorded in YPP_REG_IQC-01.

8.3Chemical and Hazardous Material Management

Hazardous chemicals and substances are identified, stored, and handled per YPP_SOP-CHM-001 and associated Safety Data Sheets (SDS). Emergency spill response is covered in YPP_SOP-ENV-003.

8.4OH&S Operational Controls

OH&S controls are established for all identified significant hazards:

  • Emergency preparedness and response — YPP_SOP-OHS-002
  • Fire safety and evacuation — YPP_SOP-OHS-003
  • Permit to Work for non-routine activities — YPP_SOP-OHS-004
  • Personal Protective Equipment requirements — defined in each WI and SOP
8.5Environmental Operational Controls

Controls for significant environmental aspects are implemented through:

  • Waste segregation and disposal — YPP_SOP-WM-001
  • Environmental emergency response — YPP_SOP-ENV-003
  • Housekeeping and spill prevention — YPP_SOP-HK-001
  • Environmental Management Programme — YPP_SOP-EMP-001
8.6Energy Operational Controls

Operational controls for Significant Energy Uses (SEUs) are defined in YPP_SOP-SEU-001. Energy data is collected monthly and tracked in YPP_REG-ENM-01. Machine operators are trained on energy-efficient operating practices.

9.0 Performance Evaluation ▾
9.1Monitoring and Measurement

Key processes are monitored against established KPIs. Performance data is collected and reviewed regularly:

  • Quality KPIs: customer complaint rate, rejection rate, on-time delivery
  • Environmental KPIs: waste to landfill (kg/month), energy consumption (kWh/tonne)
  • OH&S KPIs: Lost Time Injuries (LTI), near-miss frequency, first aid cases
  • Energy KPIs: EnPI (kWh per tonne of output), SEU consumption trends
9.2Internal Audit

A comprehensive internal audit programme (YPP_SOP-IA-001) covers all IMS clauses across all four standards at least once per audit cycle. Audit findings are categorised, communicated to process owners, and tracked to closure. Auditor independence is maintained — auditors do not audit their own work.

9.3Customer Satisfaction

Customer feedback is collected and analysed per YPP_SOP-CCF-001. Complaint trends, resolution times, and customer satisfaction scores are reviewed at Management Review Meetings.

9.4Management Review

The Director chairs a Management Review Meeting (YPP_SOP-MR-001) at least annually. Review inputs include:

  • Internal audit results and CAPA status
  • KPI performance vs. objectives
  • Customer feedback and complaint trends
  • Legal compliance status
  • Energy performance review and EnPI trends
  • Resource adequacy and opportunities for improvement
19
10.0 Improvement ▾
10.1Nonconformance and Corrective Action

All nonconformances — product defects, process deviations, OH&S incidents, environmental incidents, and audit findings — are managed through the CAPA system:

  • Document the nonconformance on NCR form — YPP_FRM-NC-01
  • Contain and correct the immediate issue
  • Perform root-cause analysis (5-Why, Fishbone, etc.)
  • Implement corrective action and verify effectiveness
  • Track all CAPAs in YPP_REG-NC-01 to closure
10.2Preventive Action and Improvement

Improvement opportunities are identified proactively through:

  • Risks identified in HIRA, EAI, and risk-based thinking reviews
  • Management review outputs and employee suggestions
  • Internal audit observations (opportunities for improvement)
  • Customer feedback, benchmarking, and energy reviews
  • Near-miss investigation findings
10.3Continual Improvement Commitment

Yash Poly Plast is committed to the continual improvement of IMS effectiveness, efficiency, and overall performance. Improvement actions are tracked, resourced, and reviewed at each Management Review Meeting to ensure they deliver the intended outcomes.

1.1उद्देश्य और अधिकार

यह आईएमएस मैनुअल यश पॉली प्लास्ट इंटीग्रेटेड मैनेजमेंट सिस्टम का शीर्ष दस्तावेज है। यह संपूर्ण सुविधा में गुणवत्ता, पर्यावरण, ओएच एंड एस और ऊर्जा प्रबंधन के लिए रूपरेखा स्थापित करता है।

  • संगठनात्मक संदर्भ, नेतृत्व प्रतिबद्धता और आईएमएस दायरे को परिभाषित करता है
  • सभी चार आईएसओ मानकों के लिए खंड-दर-खंड अनुरूपता निर्धारित करता है
  • सभी एसओपी, कार्य निर्देश, फॉर्म और रजिस्टर इस मैनुअल से अपना अधिकार प्राप्त करते हैं
  • सभी कर्मचारियों को इस ढांचे को समझना और इसके भीतर काम करना आवश्यक है
1.2दस्तावेज़ नियंत्रण

यह मैनुअल एक नियंत्रित दस्तावेज़ है. मास्टर कॉपी को प्रबंधन प्रतिनिधि (पीएल प्रधान) द्वारा इलेक्ट्रॉनिक रूप से बनाए रखा जाता है। मुद्रित प्रतियाँ तब तक अनियंत्रित होती हैं जब तक कि नियंत्रित प्रति पर मोहर न लगी हो।

4.1आंतरिक और बाहरी मुद्दे

  • बाहरी: ग्राहकों की अपेक्षाएं (अरेबियन पेट्रोलियम, अपार इंडस्ट्रीज, एक्सान इंडस्ट्रीज), कच्चे माल की कीमत में अस्थिरता
  • बाहरी: फ़ैक्टरी अधिनियम और पर्यावरण संरक्षण अधिनियम के तहत नियामक आवश्यकताएँ
  • बाहरी: प्रतिस्पर्धी परिदृश्य, सामुदायिक दायित्व और पर्यावरणीय संदर्भ
4.2इच्छुक पार्टियाँ

प्रत्येक प्रबंधन समीक्षा बैठक में निम्नलिखित इच्छुक पार्टियों और उनकी आवश्यकताओं का दस्तावेजीकरण और समीक्षा की जाती है:

  • ग्राहक - उत्पाद की गुणवत्ता, समय पर डिलीवरी, पता लगाने की क्षमता
  • कर्मचारी - सुरक्षित कार्य परिस्थितियाँ, योग्यता, उचित व्यवहार
  • विनियामक निकाय - फ़ैक्टरी अधिनियम, ईपीए, बीआईएस मानकों का अनुपालन
  • आपूर्तिकर्ता - स्पष्ट विनिर्देश, समय पर भुगतान, दीर्घकालिक साझेदारी
  • स्थानीय समुदाय - न्यूनतम पर्यावरणीय प्रभाव, शोर और अपशिष्ट
4.3आईएमएस का दायरा

आईएमएस के दायरे में शामिल हैं: ब्लो मोल्डिंग, इंजेक्शन मोल्डिंग, कच्चे माल का भंडारण, गुणवत्ता नियंत्रण, रखरखाव, भंडारण और नवी मुंबई सुविधा में प्रेषण। खंड 8.3 (डिज़ाइन और विकास) को बाहर रखा गया है क्योंकि उत्पाद ग्राहक-निर्दिष्ट डिज़ाइन के अनुसार निर्मित होते हैं।

4.4आईएमएस प्रक्रियाएं

9.1निगरानी और माप

  • गुणवत्ता KPI: ग्राहक शिकायत दर, अस्वीकृति दर, समय पर डिलीवरी
  • पर्यावरणीय KPI: लैंडफिल तक अपशिष्ट (किलो/माह), ऊर्जा खपत (kWh/टन)
  • ओएच एंड एस केपीआई: खोए हुए समय की चोटें (एलटीआई), निकट-चूक आवृत्ति, प्राथमिक चिकित्सा मामले

9.2आंतरिक लेखापरीक्षा

एक व्यापक आंतरिक लेखापरीक्षा कार्यक्रम (YPP_SOP-IA-001) प्रति लेखापरीक्षा चक्र में कम से कम एक बार सभी चार मानकों में सभी आईएमएस खंडों को शामिल करता है। ऑडिट निष्कर्षों को वर्गीकृत किया जाता है, प्रक्रिया मालिकों को सूचित किया जाता है, और समापन तक ट्रैक किया जाता है। लेखापरीक्षक की स्वतंत्रता कायम रखी जाती है - लेखापरीक्षक अपने काम का ऑडिट नहीं करते हैं।

9.3ग्राहक संतुष्टि

ग्राहक प्रतिक्रिया YPP_SOP-CCF-001 के अनुसार एकत्र और विश्लेषण की जाती है। प्रबंधन समीक्षा बैठकों में शिकायत के रुझान, समाधान समय और ग्राहक संतुष्टि स्कोर की समीक्षा की जाती है।

  • आंतरिक लेखापरीक्षा परिणाम और सीएपीए स्थिति
  • KPI कार्य परिणाम बनाम उद्देश्य

10.2निवारक कार्रवाई और सुधार

  • समीक्षा बैठक के परिणाम और कर्मचारी सुझाव

  • ग्राहक प्रतिक्रिया, बेंचमार्किंग, और ऊर्जा समीक्षाएँ
  • जांच निष्कर्ष लगभग चूक गए

Scope & Purpose ISO 9001:2015 ▾
Purpose

This procedure defines the in-process inspection frequency, parameters, acceptance criteria, and records for HDPE bottle and can manufacturing at Yash Poly Plast. It ensures that product conformance is verified at regular intervals during production to detect and contain nonconformities before they become large-scale defects.

Applies To

This procedure applies to all in-process quality checks conducted during blow moulding and injection moulding production runs for HDPE bottles and cans manufactured for Arabian Petroleum, Apar Industries, Exsan Industries, and other customers.

Responsible Parties

Quality Inspector (inspection execution and recording), Production Supervisor (corrective action on nonconformities), Production Operator (100% visual line inspection), Quality Manager / Management Representative (review of inspection trends)

2
Safety & PPE Requirements MANDATORY ▾

MANDATORY SAFETY REQUIREMENT: Use calibrated instruments only. Tag and segregate any nonconforming product immediately.

Procedure Steps ▾
#PhaseActivity / DescriptionResponsibleFlagRecord
1Sampling Plan CheckBefore commencing in-process inspection, the Quality Inspector reviews the product-specific control plan to confirm the inspection parameters, sampling frequency, acceptance criteria, and measurement instruments required for the current production run. The control plan specifies inspection frequency based on machine speed and lot size. The Quality Inspector verifies that all required instruments are available and within calibration date before starting. Any instrument found out of calibration must not be used; the Quality Manager is notified and an alternative calibrated instrument sourced. Before starting inspection, the Quality Inspector also confirms: (a) the magnetic grill is installed in the machine hopper and has been cleaned at shift start, and (b) the regrind percentage in the current material batch does not exceed 25% as per the Production Batch Record. If either condition is not met, production must be paused and the Production Supervisor notified.Quality InspectorProduct Control Plan; Calibration Register (YPP_SOP-CAL-001)
2Dimensional Inspection⚡ CCP  The Quality Inspector draws a sample per the control plan (minimum 5 units every 30 minutes or per lot, whichever is sooner) and performs dimensional measurements using calibrated instruments. Key dimensions checked: (a) Overall height and body diameter â€” vernier calliper; (b) Neck finish dimensions (outer diameter, thread profile, neck height) â€” go/no-go gauge or vernier calliper; (c) Wall thickness at body mid-point and base â€” ultrasonic thickness gauge. All measurements are compared against the product drawing tolerance. Out-of-tolerance dimensions are classified as nonconforming; the Production Supervisor is notified immediately for parameter adjustment.Quality Inspector⚡ CCPYPP_FRM-IPC-01
3Weight CheckThe Quality Inspector weighs each sample unit on a calibrated weighing scale (minimum resolution: 0.1g). The measured weight is compared against the product specification (nominal weight Â±2g, or per customer-specific tolerance on product drawing). Weight consistently below the lower tolerance limit indicates under-blown product (wall too thin â€” structural risk); weight above the upper limit indicates over-blown product (material waste). Either condition requires parameter review by the Production Supervisor. Weight results are recorded on YPP_FRM-IPC-01 alongside dimensional data.Quality InspectorYPP_FRM-IPC-01
4Visual InspectionA two-tier visual inspection is applied: (a) 100% line visual inspection by the Production Operator at the machine output point â€” each bottle/can is examined for obvious defects (flash on neck/body, heavy sink marks, short shots, severe colour streaking, or holes) before being placed in the bin/pallet. Defective pieces are placed in the rejection bin immediately. (b) Quality Inspector sample visual inspection on the drawn sample â€” examination under adequate lighting for: flash (excess plastic at parting line), sink marks, colour deviation from reference standard, surface scratches or contamination, neck deformation or incomplete thread formation, and label/print area surface quality (where applicable). Visual defect types and acceptable/rejectable criteria are defined in the visual standards chart displayed at the inspection station. Defect-to-action troubleshooting for common CMP machine defects: (i) Scratches, lines, or blemishes on blown article — check die ring (core pin) and nozzle for damage or contamination immediately; (ii) Flow lines on parison — adjust centering screws on die head body during next purge cycle; (iii) Uneven wall thickness — adjust parison straightness using A, B, C Allen bolts on die head, or review WTC profile on HMI; (iv) Surface contamination / black specks — check magnetic grill in hopper for iron particle accumulation; clean before resuming. Any recurring defect pattern must be escalated to the Production Supervisor for parameter adjustment and logged on YPP_FRM-IPC-01 with corrective action taken.Production Operator (100% line) / Quality Inspector (sample)YPP_FRM-IPC-01
5Leak / Drop Test⚡ CCP  Leak and integrity testing is performed on 5 units per lot (or per customer specification if more stringent). Two test methods are used depending on product type: (a) Pressure test â€” the bottle/can is sealed with a test cap, pressurised to the specified test pressure (per product specification), and held for the specified duration. Zero leakage is the acceptance criterion. (b) Drop test â€” the filled or capped bottle/can is dropped from a specified height (per product specification) onto a hard surface; the container must not crack, split, or leak. Test results (pass/fail per unit) are recorded on YPP_FRM-IPC-01. Any unit failing the leak or drop test triggers immediate investigation of machine parameters and a review of the full lot produced since the previous passing test.Quality Inspector⚡ CCPYPP_FRM-IPC-01
6Acceptance / Rejection Decision⚡ CCP  After completing all inspection steps, the Quality Inspector reviews the results holistically against all acceptance criteria: (a) ACCEPT â€” all parameters within specification: the production batch increment is accepted, green tag applied to the pallet/bin, and production continues. (b) HOLD â€” one or more parameters borderline out of spec, or an assignable cause is suspected: Production Supervisor is notified; machine parameters are adjusted; a re-inspection sample is taken from the next production increment before releasing on HOLD product. (c) REJECT â€” one or more parameters clearly out of specification, or a critical defect (leak failure, significant dimensional non-conformance): production is stopped, the affected lot is tagged HOLD (RED tag), and a Nonconformance Report is raised per YPP_SOP-NC-001. Rejection quantity is noted in the shift report.Quality Inspector / Production Supervisor⚡ CCPYPP_FRM-IPC-01; YPP_FRM-NC-01 (if NCR raised)
7Record and CommunicationAll in-process inspection data is recorded on YPP_FRM-IPC-01 at the time of inspection. The completed form includes: date, shift, machine ID, product code, batch number, operator, inspector name, sample size, individual measurement results, test results, overall pass/fail decision, and any corrective action taken. The Quality Inspector notifies the Production Supervisor verbally of any nonconformities immediately, and formally via the NCR if raised. At shift end, the Quality Inspector tallies total inspections, total rejections, and key defect types for the shift report. YPP_FRM-IPC-01 records are retained as quality records for a minimum of 3 years.Quality InspectorYPP_FRM-IPC-01

उद्देश्य

यह प्रक्रिया यश पॉली प्लास्ट में एचडीपीई बोतल और कैन निर्माण के लिए इन-प्रोसेस निरीक्षण आवृत्ति, पैरामीटर, स्वीकृति मानदंड और रिकॉर्ड को परिभाषित करती है। यह सुनिश्चित करता है कि बड़े पैमाने पर दोष बनने से पहले गैर-अनुरूपताओं का पता लगाने और उन्हें नियंत्रित करने के लिए उत्पादन के दौरान उत्पाद अनुरूपता को नियमित अंतराल पर सत्यापित किया जाता है।

||| पर लागू होता है यह प्रक्रिया अरेबियन पेट्रोलियम, अपार इंडस्ट्रीज, एक्सान इंडस्ट्रीज और अन्य ग्राहकों के लिए निर्मित एचडीपीई बोतलों और कैन के लिए ब्लो मोल्डिंग और इंजेक्शन मोल्डिंग उत्पादन के दौरान की जाने वाली सभी प्रक्रियागत गुणवत्ता जांच पर लागू होती है।

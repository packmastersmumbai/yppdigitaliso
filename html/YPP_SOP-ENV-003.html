Scope & Purpose ISO 14001:2015 ▾
Purpose

This procedure defines how Yash Poly Plast prepares for and responds to environmental emergencies that could cause significant environmental impact. It covers identification of potential emergency scenarios, response actions, communication, investigation, and prevention of recurrence.

Applies To

All departments and areas of the Yash Poly Plast facility including production floor, raw material and chemical stores, utilities, and waste storage areas. Applicable to employees, contractors, and visitors on site.

Responsible Parties

Management Representative (procedure ownership, mock drill coordination), Production/EHS Officer (first response), Department Heads (area-specific response), Director (external authority notification if required)

2
Safety & PPE Requirements MANDATORY ▾

MANDATORY SAFETY REQUIREMENT: In any environmental emergency, personnel safety takes priority. Evacuate if unsafe. Do not attempt to contain a spill or fire without appropriate PPE and training.

Chemical-resistant gloves
Safety goggles
Apron (for spill response)
Safety shoes

Process Flowchart — FLW-ENV-003 FLW-ENV-003 ▾
1
⚡ Critical Control PointEmergency detection: any employee who discovers an environmental emergency (spill, fire, uncontrolled emission, waste leak) immediately raises alarm and informs the EHS Officer / Management Representative.
Alert
2
⚡ Critical Control PointInitial assessment: EHS Officer assesses the scale and nature of the emergency. Determine if it can be managed internally or requires external assistance (fire brigade, MPCB, MIDC).
Assess
3
⚡ Critical Control PointContainment: for chemical/liquid spills, deploy spill kit (absorbents, containment booms). Prevent material from entering drains, soil, or water bodies. For fire with environmental impact, activate fire response per YPP_SOP-OHS-002.
Contain
4
⚡ Critical Control PointNotification: inform Director and relevant regulatory authorities (MPCB, local MIDC authority) as required by legal obligations. Record time of notification.
Notify
5
Response and cleanup: use trained personnel with appropriate PPE. Collect and dispose of contaminated material as hazardous waste per YPP_SOP-WM-001.
Respond
6
⚡ Critical Control PointInvestigation: complete YPP_FRM-ENV-02 (Environmental Incident Report) within 24 hours. Identify root cause using 5-Why or fishbone analysis.
Investigate
7
Corrective action: raise CAPA (YPP_SOP-CAPA-001) for all medium/high severity incidents. Implement preventive measures to avoid recurrence.
CAPA
8
Mock drills: conduct at least one environmental emergency mock drill per year. Record outcomes and update this procedure if gaps are identified.
Drill

उद्देश्य

यह प्रक्रिया परिभाषित करती है कि यश पॉली प्लास्ट उन पर्यावरणीय आपात स्थितियों के लिए कैसे तैयारी करता है और उन पर प्रतिक्रिया करता है जो महत्वपूर्ण पर्यावरणीय प्रभाव पैदा कर सकती हैं। इसमें संभावित आपातकालीन परिदृश्यों की पहचान, प्रतिक्रिया कार्रवाई, संचार, जांच और पुनरावृत्ति की रोकथाम शामिल है।

||| पर लागू होता है यश पॉली प्लास्ट सुविधा के सभी विभाग और क्षेत्र जिनमें उत्पादन मंजिल, कच्चे माल और रासायनिक भंडार, उपयोगिताएँ और अपशिष्ट भंडारण क्षेत्र शामिल हैं। कर्मचारियों, ठेकेदारों और साइट पर आगंतुकों के लिए लागू।

जिम्मेदार पार्टियाँ

प्रबंधन प्रतिनिधि (प्रक्रिया स्वामित्व, मॉक ड्रिल समन्वय), उत्पादन/ईएचएस अधिकारी (पहली प्रतिक्रिया), विभाग प्रमुख (क्षेत्र-विशिष्ट प्रतिक्रिया), निदेशक (यदि आवश्यक हो तो बाहरी प्राधिकरण अधिसूचना)
